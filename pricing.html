Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my automation runs?

We'll notify you at 80% usage. Overage is billed at $0.01 per run. For consistent overages, we'll recommend upgrading to a better-fit plan.

Do I own the automations you build?

100%. You own all code, workflows, documentation, and data. No vendor lock-in. Ever.

What's included in "strategy consultation"?

60-minute calls with our strategists to discuss automation opportunities, review analytics, plan roadmaps, or get expert advice on AI and intelligence topics.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime and you'll have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer discounts for annual payment?

Yes! Pay annually and get 2 months free (16.7% discount). Contact us to set up annual billing.

What if I need something not in my plan?

No problem. We can add one-off services à la carte or customize a plan that fits your specific needs. Just ask.

Do you offer a free trial?

We offer something better: a free discovery call where we'll analyze your business, identify opportunities, and provide a custom proposal. Zero risk, zero obligation.
