About Intelastart

Founded by entrepreneurs who believe AI should work for you, not the other way around.

Our Story

Intelastart was born from frustration. As startup founders ourselves, we watched businesses struggle with the same problems: drowning in manual tasks, making decisions without data, and unable to compete with larger, better-funded competitors.

We knew AI could solve these problems — but most solutions were either too expensive, too complex, or designed for enterprises with dedicated data science teams.

So we built Intelastart: AI-powered business intelligence and automation designed specifically for startups, small businesses, and ambitious entrepreneurs who want enterprise-level capabilities without enterprise-level budgets or complexity.

Our Mission

"Democratize business intelligence and automation so every entrepreneur can compete like a Fortune 500 company."
